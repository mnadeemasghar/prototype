Staff Lead Creation Flow

Alsultania Travel CRM & Management System

flowchart TD
  A[User navigates to Leads module] --> B{How did they arrive?}
  B -->|Inbound call / WhatsApp| C[Phone number pre-filled in header]
  B -->|Manual from Leads list| D[Open Create Lead — phone may be entered separately]
  C --> E[Create Lead screen loads]
  D --> E
  E --> F[Previous Leads panel loads for phone number]
  F --> G[User fills Customer Name + Lead Type]
  G --> H[Dynamic section renders for selected Lead Type]
  H --> I[User completes Dates and Travelers]
  I --> J[Optional: Remarks]
  J --> K[User selects Lead Handling option]
  K --> L{Handling choice}
  L -->|Takeover| M[Lead assigned to current user]
  L -->|Create for Others| N[User selects target agent]
  L -->|Transfer to Staff| O[Lead queued for staff transfer]
  M --> P[Click CREATE NEW LEAD]
  N --> P
  O --> P
  P --> Q[System validates + saves lead]
  Q --> R[Lead appears in list / assigned handler inbox]
